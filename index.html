01. about

Welcome to UX_FESTIVAL.

A fully immersive audio/visual experience like you have never seen before.

UX_FESTIVAL was founded and created by EVE and EIGHT in 2022. Their goal was to create a virtual event that had the potential to put undiscovered and under appreciated talent from across the internet in the spotlight and provide a platform for these individuals to showcase their capabilities as artists.

Having previously performed at various online festivals such as ABYSS Festival, Digital Nightmare and Bishu's Morefest, EVE set out on a journey to create an online festival that focussed on virtual immersion and audience engagement in the most creative way possible.

Now running for the third year in a row with UX_003, UX_FESTIVAL and it's team is driven towards creating one of the most immersive audio/visual experiences within the online music community!
